## Contact Us

If you need support or have questions about WebHotelier or wplugged, please get in touch.
You can reach us using the contact form on the live site or via the support
email used by WPlugged. If you prefer, add a contact email here (e.g.
`support@wplugged.com`) or embed a form block.

The live site contact form fields typically include:

- Name
- Email
- Message

Submit the form to contact support.
